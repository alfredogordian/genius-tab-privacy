🛡️ Our Privacy Commitment

Genius Tab is built with privacy as our top priority. We believe your browsing data should remain private and under your control.

📊 Data Collection

We collect ZERO user data. Genius Tab does not:

  • Collect any personally identifiable information
  • Track your browsing history or activity
  • Send any data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Store any information outside your local device

🔍 How It Works

Genius Tab processes information locally in your browser to organize tabs:

  • Tab Analysis: Reads tab titles and URLs locally to group similar content
  • Bookmark Access: Reads your bookmark structure to organize tabs by folders
  • Local Storage: Saves your preferences only on your device

All processing happens locally - no data leaves your browser.

🔐 Permissions Explained

  • tabs: To read tab titles/URLs for grouping (processed locally)
  • tabGroups: To create and manage tab groups
  • storage: To save your settings locally
  • bookmarks: To organize tabs by bookmark folders
  • activeTab: To identify current tab for better organization

🌐 Third-Party Services

Genius Tab does not use any third-party services, analytics, or external APIs. The extension is completely self-contained.

🔄 Updates

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify users of any significant changes through extension updates.
